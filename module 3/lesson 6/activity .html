Neymar Jr. is a Brazilian football superstar born in 1992, renowned for his dazzling dribbling and flair. Rising from Santos FC, he joined FC Barcelona in 2013, winning the treble as part of the famous "MSN" trio. In 2017, he made a record-breaking transfer to PSG. He led Brazil to 2016 Olympic Gold and became the nation's all-time top scorer.
